What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or phone) when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and understand how you use them.

You can control and manage cookies in several ways. Most browsers allow you to refuse or delete cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

To opt out of Google Analytics tracking specifically, you can install the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.
