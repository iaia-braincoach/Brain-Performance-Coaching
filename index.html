About Me

Iaia Colella
Brain Performance Coach

With a background in psychology and cognitive neuroscience — including a Master's from Brunel University London — I've spent years studying how the brain drives performance under pressure.

I work with national and international athletes across track & field, swimming, powerlifting, and more. My approach combines neuroscience, NLP, and mental performance training to close the gap between training and competition.

I've proven these methods on myself — completing a half marathon with no prior distance training, powered entirely by mental preparation. If the mind can do that, imagine what it unlocks for athletes already training their bodies.

My Mission
To make brain training as important as physical training in every athlete's programme — giving you the tools to feel confident, clear, and purposeful when it counts.
The Method

8-Dimension Method™

A neuroscience-based framework developed from psychology and cognitive neuroscience to understand and strengthen your mental performance.

This is the foundation of every coaching programme — mapping exactly where your mental game is holding you back, across the 8 dimensions that determine how you compete.
