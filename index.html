About Me

Iaia Colella

Brain Performance Coach

I’m Iaia Colella, a Brain Performance Coach with a background in psychology, neuroscience and sport performance. I help athletes train their mind with the same structure, discipline and intensity they use to train their body, so they can compete with clarity, confidence and control.

Before becoming a coach, I spent years studying human behaviour, performance and the mental patterns that shape how we think, feel and act under pressure. Through my BSc in Psychology and my MSc in Clinical & Cognitive Neuroscience at Brunel University London, I discovered one truth that drives all my work today: your brain is trainable and mental performance is a skill you can build.

I’ve worked with athletes across different sports, helping them overcome mental blocks, regulate emotions, build confidence and perform consistently at their highest potential.

My approach is structured, scientific and personalised. I don’t teach generic “mindset”. I help athletes build mental systems, cognitive habits and performance routines that work when the pressure is highest: in training, in competition and in recovery.

I founded Brain Performance Coaching to give athletes what’s often missing in traditional training: a place to strengthen the mind as deliberately as the body, develop resilience, and perform with unwavering focus.

If you’re ready to elevate your mental game, unlock your potential and become the athlete you know you can be, I’d love to work with you.

8-DIMENSION METHOD™

A structured approach to mental performance

A neuroscience-based framework to understand and strengthen your mental performance. The 8-Dimension Assessment is the foundation of my coaching approach.

Developed from psychology and cognitive neuroscience, it evaluates eight key areas that influence performance:

  • Focus & Attention: Ability to maintain concentration during training and competition.
  • Emotional Regulation: Managing stress, anxiety and emotional responses under pressure.
  • Confidence & Self-Belief: Trusting your skills and preparation in high-stakes situations.
  • Motivation & Goal Setting: Setting clear objectives and sustaining drive throughout the season.
  • Resilience & Coping: Bouncing back from setbacks and maintaining mental toughness.
  • Visualization & Imagery: Using mental rehearsal to enhance performance and prepare for competition.
  • Decision Making & Problem Solving: Making quick, effective choices during dynamic situations.
  • Recovery & Mindfulness: Techniques to relax, recover and maintain mental clarity.

Every athlete I work with starts with this assessment, as the baseline of their mental performance profile. It helps us identify strengths, patterns, blind spots and areas for growth.
