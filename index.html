About Me

Iaia Colella

Brain Performance Coach


I’m a Brain Performance Coach with a background in psychology and cognitive neuroscience. I help athletes train their brain with structure and intention, so they can perform with confidence and consistency.

Before coaching, I spent years studying human behaviour, high-performance psychology and the mental processes that influence athletes under pressure. My approach is scientific, practical and personalised.

I’ve supported athletes across multiple sports, helping them regulate emotions, build stronger confidence, stay focused, and bounce back from setbacks.

I founded Brain Performance Coaching to give athletes the tool that was missing in traditional training: a structured way to develop strong mental skills for training, competition and life.

My Mission

"My mission is to make brain training an essential part of every athlete’s journey. I want athletes to recognise the power of training their mind with the same commitment they give to their body. Through this work, I empower athletes to perform with confidence, clarity and purpose — in sport and in life."

8-DIMENSION METHOD™

A neuroscience-based framework to understand and strengthen your mental performance.

The 8-Dimension Assessment is the foundation of my coaching approach.

Developed from psychology and cognitive neuroscience, it evaluates eight key areas that influence performance:


  • Focus & Attention: Ability to maintain concentration during training and competition.
  • Emotional Regulation: Managing stress, anxiety and emotional responses under pressure.
  • Confidence & Self-Belief: Trusting your skills and preparation in high-stakes situations.
  • Motivation & Goal Setting: Setting clear objectives and sustaining drive throughout the season.
  • Resilience & Coping: Bouncing back from setbacks and maintaining mental toughness.
  • Visualization & Imagery: Using mental rehearsal to enhance performance and prepare for competition.
  • Decision Making & Problem Solving: Making quick, effective choices during dynamic situations.
  • Recovery & Mindfulness: Techniques to relax, recover and maintain mental clarity.
